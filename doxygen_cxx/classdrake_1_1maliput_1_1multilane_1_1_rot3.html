Detailed Description

An R^3 rotation parameterized by roll, pitch, yaw.

This effects a compound rotation around space-fixed x-y-z axes:

Rot3(roll, pitch, yaw) * V = RotZ(yaw) * RotY(pitch) * RotX(roll) * V
