Detailed Description

Evaluates the joint limit constraint force.

For a single joint (revolute or prismatic), whose index in the velocity vector is i, its joint limit force has the form [0, 0, ..., 0, -λᵤ+λₗ, 0, ... ,0], that only the i'th entry is non-zero. where λᵤ / λₗ are the joint limit force from upper / lower limit respectively. We assume that both λᵤ and λₗ are non-negative.
