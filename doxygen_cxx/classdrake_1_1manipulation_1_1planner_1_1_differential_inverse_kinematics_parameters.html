)

Constructor.

Initializes the nominal joint position to zeros of size num_positions. Timestep is initialized to 1. The end effector gains are initialized to ones. All constraints are initialized to nullopt.

Parameters
num_positionsNumber of generalized positions.
num_velocitiesNumber of generalized velocities.
